Who Are We?

We are Electronics Engineer Students' Association of Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli. We have 84 currently members in our association. We have a rich legacy of 28 years.



What Do We Aim?

We aim to create technical as well as non-technical excellence in our club members. We are always in search of increasing the team potential and hence making students ready to face real life career challenges.

Our Programmes

Throughout the academic year, we arrange various activities.

Intraclub Events

We arrange competitions where members can apply their knowledge, leadership skills and interpersonal skills.

Club Services

We arrange various club services where we provide technical and non-technical knowledge to students.

Technical Workshops

We arrange special workshops on various topics to help students learn more.

What Do We Promote?

  Leadership

  Team Work

  Management

It is important to learn about management and discipline. Members often support each other on and off the field, helping one another keep track of schedules and academic responsibilities. Great management skill is must in this era.
